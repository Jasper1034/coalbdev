Income & Budgeting

Manage your income and expenses with the Income & Budgeting section. Create personalized budgets based on your income, prioritize your spending, and track your savings goals.

By keeping track of your income and setting up budgets for each category of spending, you'll gain better control over your finances and ensure that you're always living within your means.
