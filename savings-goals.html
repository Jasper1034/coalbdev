Savings & Goals

Setting financial goals and tracking your progress is crucial for achieving financial stability. With this tool, you can set up savings goals for specific needs, such as emergency funds, vacations, or major purchases.

It helps you stay on track and motivated, turning your dreams into achievable goals with a clear savings plan in place.
