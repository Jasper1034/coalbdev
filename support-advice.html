Support & Advice

We are here to assist you with troubleshooting, tips, and support for managing your finances through the WLV Finance App.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I add an expense?

To add an expense, navigate to the "Daily Expenses" section, fill out the form with the required information (category, amount, and date), and click "Add Expense".

How can I set a budget?

Go to the "Set Your Budget Goal" section and enter the amount you'd like to budget for. Then click "Set Budget". You'll receive notifications if you exceed your budget.

Can I track recurring expenses?

Currently, the app allows for manual tracking of daily expenses. However, recurring expenses are a feature we are working on for future updates.

Contact Support

If you need further assistance, feel free to reach out to our support team. We're here to help!

Financial Advice & Tips

Here are some tips on how to manage your finances effectively:

  • Track Your Spending: Always keep an eye on where your money is going. Use the "Daily Expenses" feature to log every transaction.
  • Set a Budget: Setting a budget helps you stay within your limits. Try to stick to your budget and adjust as necessary.
  • Save for the Future: It's important to allocate some money towards savings. Try setting up a monthly savings goal.
  • Review Regularly: Regularly review your spending and adjust your budget to ensure you're on track with your financial goals.
