Daily Expenses

Track your daily spending habits with ease! Categorize your purchases and gain insights into where your money goes. This helps you identify areas where you can cut back and save.

With this tool, you'll be able to effectively track your expenses in real-time, categorize purchases (e.g., food, transportation, entertainment), and see a breakdown of your spending patterns over time.
